Finally, to verify the Dead Letter Queue (DLQ) functionality, we can send an invalid order (for example, omitting the required productId in the payload). The service will reject it, and it will be routed to the DLQ:

curl -u guest:guest -H "content-type:application/json" -X POST http://localhost:15672/api/exchanges/%2f/order.events/publish -d '{
  "properties": {"content_type": "application/json"},
  "routing_key": "order.placed",
  "payload": "{\"orderId\":\"error-order-002\",\"productId\":null,\"quantity\":1,\"customerId\":\"cust-42\",\"timestamp\":\"2026-05-24T12:00:00Z\"}",
  "payload_encoding": "string"
}'

docker compose down
